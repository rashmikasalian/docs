## 🧠 What is Recruit Edge?

**Recruit Edge** is Mantrika.ai’s AI-powered recruitment agent—designed to radically transform how organizations **attract**, **acquire**, and **assimilate** external talent.

Built on our proprietary **SWTT Framework** and **Digital Talent Twin**, Recruit Edge replaces manual, siloed hiring tools with an intelligent, end-to-end decision engine that:
- Delivers hyper-personalized candidate experiences
- Predicts fit, readiness, and offer acceptance
- Reduces drop-offs and time-to-hire
- Aligns hiring with long-term enterprise value

Whether you’re hiring at scale or building niche teams, Recruit Edge ensures **every hire is a strategic move**.

## ⚙️ Capabilities by Stage: The 3A Framework

Recruit Edge structures every capability around the **3A framework**, capturing the full external recruitment journey.

---

### 1️⃣ ATTRACT – Intelligent Talent Discovery

Identify and excite top-fit candidates before they even apply.

**Core Features**:
- 🌍 **Location Intelligence**  
  Visualize skill supply, growth rate, salary benchmarks, and DEI ratios with our **Location Favorability Index (LFI)**.

- 🧠 **AI-Generated Job Descriptions**  
  Auto-create SEO-optimized, role-specific listings with inclusive language.

- 📢 **Branding & EVP Optimization**  
  Align job ads with your culture, values, and DEI storytelling.

- 🛰️ **Diversity-Aware Smart Sourcing**  
  Engage passive candidates using targeted platforms, career communities, and DEI-calibrated outreach.

- 🧬 **Unified Candidate Profiling**  
  Merge resumes, assessments, and behavioral data into one **Digital Talent Twin**.

---

### 2️⃣ ACQUIRE – Precision Screening & Fit Matching

Streamline evaluations, predict fit, and close with confidence.

**Core Features**:
- 🔍 **AI Resume Parsing + Soft-Skill Scoring**  
  Extract hard + soft skills using NLP, tone analysis, and context-aware relevance.

- 🎥 **AI-Led Interviews**  
  Adaptive video interviews with communication scoring, bias mitigation, and explainability.

- 🧠 **SWTT Fit Modeling**  
  Match candidates by Skill × Will × Task × Time for optimal alignment.

- 📊 **Skill-Gap Radar Visualization**  
  Radar charts comparing candidate proficiency vs role benchmarks.

- 💬 **Smart Interview Collaboration**  
  AI-generated kits, real-time feedback, and final recommendation layers.

- 📄 **Offer Scenario Modeling + Predictability**  
  Predict offer acceptance likelihood, simulate multiple compensation options, and send nudges through the system.

---

### 3️⃣ ASSIMILATE – Personalized Onboarding & Ramp-Up

Ensure new hires succeed from Day 0.

**Core Features**:
- 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **AI Buddy + Nudges**  
  Conversational agent that shares onboarding content, answers FAQs, and monitors engagement.

- 🎮 **Gamified Preboarding**  
  Learning journeys, light assessments, and micro-challenges to build excitement.

- 📅 **90-Day Smart Ramp-Up Blueprint**  
  Customized onboarding mapped to milestones and task alignment.

- 🔐 **Blockchain-Based BGV**  
  Tamper-proof document verification, identity checks, and fraud detection.

- 🔁 **Loop Closure & Talent Recycling**  
  Personalized rejections, role rematching, and nurture pools for future-fit talent.
